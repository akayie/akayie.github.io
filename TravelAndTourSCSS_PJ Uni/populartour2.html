Yangon - Bagan – Nyaung U – Popa - Yangon Tour

Overviews

Per adult = 350,000/- Kyats (2 or 3 people sharing)

    Itineraries

    Day 1

  • Depart Yangon at 5 am. After breakfast, continue your journey to 115 miles. After lunch at a local restaurant, continue your journey to Bagan. In the evening, arrive in Bagan and check-in at the hotel. After dinner at the hotel with a set menu, rest peacefully at the hotel

    • Day 2

  • After breakfast at the hotel, start visiting the Bagan pagodas. Visit Manuha Pagoda, Sabba Nyunt Pagoda, Kanto Palam Pagoda, Ananda Pagoda. After lunch at a clay pot restaurant, return to the hotel and rest briefly. In the evening, visit Dhammarangyi Pagoda, Visit the Sulamani Pagoda, Pyasada Gyi (Pyasada Gyi) and other pagodas. After dinner at a local restaurant, return to the hotel and take a rest.

    • Day 3

  • After breakfast at the hotel, visit Poppa Daung. After lunch at a Burmese buffet, return to the hotel and take a rest. In the evening, visit Shwe Si Khon Pagoda, Alotaw Pyae Gu Pagoda, Htilo Min Lo Pagoda, and Bu Pagoda. After dinner at a local restaurant, return to the hotel and take a rest.

    • Day 4

  • After breakfast at the hotel, visit the five full-fledged pagodas: Lokananda, Tamane Thein, Shwe San Daw, and (2) Dharmarajaka Pagoda. After lunch at a local restaurant, return to the hotel and take a rest. In the evening, we will enter the Paingtan Forest and drink sweet palm juice and buy local souvenirs. After dinner, we will return to Yangon.

    • Day 5

  • Return to Yangon early in the morning and enjoy a pleasant trip.
