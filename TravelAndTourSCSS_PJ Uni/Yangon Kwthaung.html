Yangon – Kawthaung – Awei Pila – Kawthaung

Overviews

Per adult = 350,000/- Kyats (2 or 3 people sharing)

    Itineraries

    Day 1

    Yangon - Kawthaung - Awei Pila

  • We will fly from Yangon to Kawthaung. After lunch at a local restaurant in Kawthaung, we will continue to Awei Pila Island by Speed ​​Boat. We will check-in at Awei Pila Hotel and take a short break. After dinner at the hotel, we will have a peaceful sleep.    

    • Day 2

    Awei Pila

  • Breakfast will be served at the hotel. You can participate in the fun activities arranged by the hotel (Snorkeling, Kayaking, Jungle trekking to fisherman's Village). Lunch and dinner will be served at the hotel. You will sleep peacefully at Awei Pila Hotel.   

    • Day 3

    Awei Pila

  • Breakfast will be served at the hotel. You can participate in the fun activities arranged by the hotel (Snorkeling, Kayaking, Jungle trekking to fisherman's Village). Lunch and dinner will be served at the hotel. You will sleep peacefully at Awei Pila Hotel. 

    • Day 4

    Awei Pila - Kawthaung - Yangon

  • After breakfast at the hotel, check out. From the hotel, we will depart by speed boat to Kawthaung Marina. We will then be transferred by car to Kawthaung Airport and then depart by domestic flight to Yangon, ending our enjoyable journey.
