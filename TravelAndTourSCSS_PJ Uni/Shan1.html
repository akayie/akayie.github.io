Yangon – Kalaw – Ywangan – Yangon

Overviews

Per adult = 350,000/- Kyats (2 or 3 people sharing)

    Itineraries

    Day 1

  • Depart Yangon at 5 am. After breakfast, continue your journey to 250 miles. After lunch at a local restaurant, continue your journey to Kalaw. In the evening, arrive in Kalaw and check-in at the hotel. After dinner at the hotel with a set menu, rest peacefully at the hotel

    • Day 2

  • After breakfast at the hotel, start visiting the Kalaw Highlights. Visit Byike Mountain, Chit Thu Lan Kyar, Kalaw City View. After lunch at a clay pot restaurant, return to the hotel and rest briefly. In the evening, depart to the Ywangan.

    • Day 3

  • After breakfast at the hotel, visit Ta Lote Cha Waterfall. After lunch at a Burmese buffet, return to the hotel and take a rest. In the evening, visit Shwe Moke Htaw Pagoda,Shwe Daung Eu Pagoda,Mya Paw Saw Pagoda and Moe Ma Kha Pagoda. After dinner at a local restaurant, return to the hotel and take a rest.

    • Day 4

  • After breakfast at the hotel, visit Than Myo Mountain,Sin Lal Natural Cave,Sin Sar Pyar Village Camp Site. After lunch at a local restaurant, return to the hotel and take a rest. In the evening, we will enter Yae Oo Five Day Market and buy local souvenirs. After dinner, we will return to Yangon.

    • Day 5

  • Return to Yangon early in the morning and enjoy a pleasant trip.

hotels Comparison

Kalaw Heritage Hotel since 1903
Surrounded by gardens, this genteel 1903 hotel set in 3 British Colonial-style buildings is 2 km from street food at the Kalaw Myoma Market and 3 km from city views at Thein Taung Monastery. It's 1 km from the Kalaw train station.

Pine Hill Resort
Among pine trees and mountains, this modern resort is a 13-minute walk from Kalaw Railway Station and 1 mile from Aung Chan Tha Zedi Buddhist temple.

360 Kalaw Hotel
In a leafy neighbourhood, this relaxed hotel is a kilometre from both Kalaw Railway Station and Shwe Oo Min Monastery. Kalaw Town Market is 2 km away.

Hillock & Honeymoon Villa-Bed & Breakfast
On a leafy hillside surrounded by homes, this humble B&B in a chalet-style building is a 9-minute walk from Kalaw train station and 2 km from the Thein Taung Pagoda Monastery.
