Yangon – Sagaing – Mingun – Yangon

Overviews

Per adult = 350,000/- Kyats (2 or 3 people sharing)

    Itineraries

    Day 1

  • Depart Yangon at 8 am. After breakfast, continue your journey to Sagaing.Lanch was held at a local restaruant and arrived in Sagaing in the evening and check-in hotel.After dinner at the hotel with a set menu, rest peacefully at the hotel

    • Day 2

  • After breakfast at the hotel, start visiting the Sagaing Highlights. Visit November Garden,Architectural Buildings , Sagaing Hill , Bodhi Tataung ,Ava Bridge , Phowintaung, Kambazathadi Golden Palace,U Min Thonze Caves and Hsinbyume Pagoda. After lunch at a clay pot restaurant, return to the hotel and rest briefly. In the evening, depart to the Mingun.

    • Day 3

  • After breakfast at the hotel, visit Nay Tat Pagoda. After lunch at a Burmese buffet, return to the hotel and take a rest. In the evening, visit Mingun Bell,Mingun Pahtodawgyi, Hsinbyume Pagoda, and Mingun Pagoda.. After dinner at a local restaurant, return to the hotel and take a rest.

    • Day 4

  • After breakfast at the hotel, visit some places. After lunch at a local restaurant, return to the hotel and take a rest. In the evening, we will buy local souvenirs. After dinner, we will return to Yangon.

    • Day 5

  • Return to Yangon early in the morning and enjoy a pleasant trip.
