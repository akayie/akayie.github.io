Yangon – Ngapali – Yangon

Overviews

Per adult = 350,000/- Kyats (2 or 3 people sharing)

    Itineraries

    Day 1

    Yangon - Ngapali

  • We will start our journey from Yangon at around 8pm (8pm) in front of Aung San Stadium. We will meet our guests at various stops along the way. During the Lock Down, we will stop briefly and sleep in the car.  

    • Day 2

    Ngapali

  • We will depart at 4:00 AM and arrive at Ngapali Beach early in the morning. We will have breakfast at a local restaurant on Ngapali Beach, check-in at the hotel and rest. We will have lunch and dinner at a local restaurant.  

    • Day 3

    Ngapali

  • Breakfast at the hotel and start the island tour. Visit Nyaungpin Island Beach, Blue Lagoon, Floating Bridge, etc. and have lunch at Paradise Restaurant. Continue to visit Thae Phyu Island, Light House, Pearl Island and do some snorkeling. In the evening, return to the hotel and relax. After dinner at a local restaurant, you will have a peaceful night's sleep at the hotel. 

    • Day 4

    Ngapali - Yangon

  • Breakfast at the hotel and check-out from the hotel. We will also visit the Mya Yadana Pyay Pagoda, the Rak Pyay Pagoda on the top of the mountain, and the three famous pagodas of Thandwe, namely An Taw, San Taw, and Nam Taw. After shopping for local souvenirs at Thandwe Market, we will depart for Yangon. Lunch will be served at a local restaurant and we will continue our journey to Yangon. We will return to Yangon in the early evening and enjoy a pleasant trip.
