Yangon – Taunggyi – Inle Lake – Yangon

Overviews

Per adult = 350,000/- Kyats (2 or 3 people sharing)

    Itineraries

    Day 1

  • Depart Yangon at 5 am. After breakfast, continue your journey to Taunggyi.Lanch was held at a local restaruant and arrived in Taunggyi in the evening and check-in hotel.After dinner at the hotel with a set menu, rest peacefully at the hotel

    • Day 2

  • After breakfast at the hotel, start visiting the Taunggyi Highlights. Visit Kakku Pagoda,Golden Blossoms and Great Glory and Fortune Pagoda. After lunch at a clay pot restaurant, return to the hotel and rest briefly. In the evening, depart to the Inle Lake.

    • Day 3

  • After breakfast at the hotel, visit Nga Phe Chaung Monastery. After lunch at a Burmese buffet, return to the hotel and take a rest. In the evening, visit Phaung Daw Oo Pagoda,Kyaung Daw Pagoda. After dinner at a local restaurant, return to the hotel and take a rest.

    • Day 4

  • After breakfast at the hotel, visit Jumping Cat Monastery,Inn Oo Ancient Pagoda,Phaung Daw Pout Cave. After lunch at a local restaurant, return to the hotel and take a rest. In the evening, we will enter Natural Hot Spring and buy local souvenirs. After dinner, we will return to Yangon.

    • Day 5

  • Return to Yangon early in the morning and enjoy a pleasant trip.

UCT Taunggyi Hotel
Featuring views of the surrounding mountains, this casual budget hotel along a main road is 4 minutes' walk from Cultural Museum (Taunggyi), 6 km from Shwe Bone Pwint Pagoda and 40 km from Heho Airport.

Mountain Star Hotel
Set on a busy road lined with shops and eateries, this informal hotel is 6 km from Shwe Bone Pwint Pagoda and 39 km from Heho Airport.

On a hillside with views of Taunggyi city and the Shan Hills, this upscale hotel is 4 km from Shwe Bone Pwint Pagoda and 2 km from Taunggyi railway station.
