Yangon – Panglong – Loilem – Yangon

Overviews

Per adult = 350,000/- Kyats (2 or 3 people sharing)

    Itineraries

    Day 1

  • Depart Yangon at 5 am. After breakfast, continue your journey to Penglong.Lanch was held at a local restaruant and arrived in Penglong in the evening and check-in hotel.After dinner at the hotel with a set menu, rest peacefully at the hotel

    • Day 2

  • After breakfast at the hotel, start visiting the Penglong Highlights. Visit Wan En Monastery,Sin Swel Pan Cave. After lunch at a clay pot restaurant, return to the hotel and rest briefly. In the evening, depart to the Loilem.

    • Day 3

  • After breakfast at the hotel, visit Buddha Pagoda Hill. After lunch at a Burmese buffet, return to the hotel and take a rest. In the evening, visit Dhamma Zay Ti,Mwe Taw Pagoda. After dinner at a local restaurant, return to the hotel and take a rest.

    • Day 4

  • After breakfast at the hotel, visit Buddhist temple,Mya Kan Thar Garden. After lunch at a local restaurant, return to the hotel and take a rest. In the evening, we will enter Natural Hot Spring and buy local souvenirs. After dinner, we will return to Yangon.

    • Day 5

  • Return to Yangon early in the morning and enjoy a pleasant trip.
