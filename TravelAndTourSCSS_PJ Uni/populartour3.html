Yangon – Zwegabin – Kyaik Hteee Yoe – Yangon

Overviews

Per adult = 350,000/- Kyats (2 or 3 people sharing)

    Itineraries

    Day 1

  • Depart Yangon at 6 am. After breakfast at a local restaurant, drive to Hpa-An. Arrive in Hpa-An and have lunch at a local restaurant and check-in at hotel. After dinner at the hotel with a set menu, rest peacefully at the hotel .

    • Day 2

  • After breakfast at the hotel, start hiking to Zwegabin Mountain.Enjoy panoramic views from the top and visit Zwegabin Monastery and feed the monkeys.Descend the mountain and have lunch at a clay pot restaurant,then return to the hotel and rest briefly. In the evening, depart to the Kyaik Htee Yoee.

    • Day 3

  • Arrive at Kinpun Camp and take an open truck to Golden Rock,and then check-in at a hotel near the pagoda.After breakfast at the hotel, explore the Golden Rock Pagoda and enjoy the breathtaking view.After lunch at a Burmese buffet, return to the hotel and take a rest. In the evening, enjoy the sunset at the Golden Rock. After dinner at a local restaurant, return to the hotel and take a rest.

    • Day 4

  • After breakfast at the hotel,descend back to Kinpun Base Camp. After lunch at a local restaurant, return to the hotel and take a rest. In the evening, drive to Bago. After Lunch at Bago,visits ShweMawDaw Pagoda and KanBawZaThaDi Palace.After dinner, we will return to Yangon.

    • Day 5

  • Return to Yangon early in the morning and enjoy a pleasant trip.
