Yangon – Myeik – Yangon

Overviews

Per adult = 350,000/- Kyats (2 or 3 people sharing)

    Itineraries

    Day 1

    Yangon - Myeik

  • Depart from Yangon at 8:00 PM from Aung San Stadium. Pick up guests at designated stops along the way. Travel overnight by car/bus and take short breaks during the journey.

    • Day 2

    Myeik (City & Nearby Attractions)

  • Arrive in Myeik around 4:00 AM and have breakfast at a local restaurant. Check-in at the hotel and take a short rest.

    • Day 3

    Island Tour (Done Island & Smart Island)

  •  Have breakfast and depart for an island-hopping tour. Take a boat to Done Island (1.5hr boat ride). Enjoy snorkeling, diving, and kayaking in the crystal-clear waters. Have lunch at Paradise Restaurant on the island. Visit Smart Island to explore coral reefs and birdwatching spots. Return to Myeik in the evening, relax at the hotel, and enjoy dinner at a local restaurant.

    • Day 4

    Island Tour (Kyunn Mee & Langann Island)

  • Explore coral reefs, enjoy snorkeling, and relax in the peaceful atmosphere. Visit Langann Island, home to secret beaches and amazing kayaking spots. Return to Myeik in the evening and explore the night market before dinner.

    • Day 5

    Myeik - Yangon

  • Enjoy a traditional Myeik breakfast (Butter Roti, Mohinga, etc.). Shop for local souvenirs at Myeik Market. Depart for Yangon after lunch at a local restaurant. Arrive in Yangon around 6:00 - 8:00 PM, ending a memorable trip.
