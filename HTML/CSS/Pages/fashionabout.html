Fashion is a dynamic force that ignites our sense of self-worth, enhances our creativity, and reflects our inner beauty. Its importance lies not only in how it makes us look but also in how it makes us feel.

2. What is the purpose of fashion?
Fashion is also a source of art, allowing people to display their unique tastes, sensibilities, and styles. Different fashion designers are influenced by outside stimuli and reflect this inspiration in their work.
