    Itineraries

    Day 1

  • We will depart from Yangon at around 6 am . We will have breakfast in Nyaung Don and then proceed to Ngwe Saung Beach. After lunch, we will have lunch at a local restaurant in Ngwe Saung and then check-in at the hotel. After dinner, we will have a peaceful night ’s sleep at the hotel.

    • Day 2

  • After breakfast at the hotel, you will have a peaceful rest while enjoying the beauty of the beach. Lunch and dinner will be served at a local restaurant in Ngwesaung before you sleep peacefully at the hotel.

    • Day 3

  • After breakfast at the hotel , we will check out of the hotel at around 11:00 AM and depart for Yangon. We will have lunch in Pathein and continue our journey. We will return to Yangon peacefully in the evening, ending  our enjoyable trip.  

    • Day 4

  • The bus will depart from Yangon at around 5 am. Breakfast and lunch will be served at a local restaurant . In the afternoon , check-in at Mangrove Resort in Ngwe Thaung Yan . Then, we will have a peaceful night's sleep at the hotel.

    • Day 5

    • Breakfast will be served at the hotel. Activities available at Mangrove Resort include swimming in the swimming pool , cycling in the garden , boating in the mangrove bridge , kayaking in the clean and beautiful creek , riding in the Sea and Lake buggy , playing water games at the private beach, and exciting activities such as Quick Jump and Climbing . Then, we will relax and sleep peacefully at the hotel. After breakfast at the hotel, check-out . Depart for Yangon in the afternoon and arrive back in Yangon in the evening.
